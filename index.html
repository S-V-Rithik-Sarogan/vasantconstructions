Who We Are

Nila Builders, rooted in Chennai since 2016, crafts homes and offices that blend Tamil tradition with modern comfort. Our name, inspired by the moon, reflects our commitment to lasting quality.

With a 35-member team, including RERA-registered architects and Ayadi-Vastu experts, we’ve delivered over 200 projects across Tamil Nadu. We’re BIS-compliant and support local initiatives like Chennai Temple Trust.

Our Vision: Quality construction with Tamil heritage at heart.
